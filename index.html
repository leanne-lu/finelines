about fine lines -

FINE LINES is a life drawing class based in Manchester. Birthed from an online lockdown project, FINE LINES has grown into a community filled with creativity, networking, fun and exploration. Ness & Lea-Lea use this space as one of their endless creative outlets, one to bring people together.
